White Rice

Description

I'm sure you're hungry. Yes you are, aren't you?
So, today, I'll be teaching you how to cook the main food in asia, RICE!

Ingredients

  • 250g Rice
  • 375ml Water

Steps

  1. First of all, take all the rice in a net, and wash the rice. Then, put them into a cooking pot. pour the drinking water we kept for cooking in the pot.
  2. Turn on the stove, and place the pot on it. Leave it to boil for 20 minutes.
  3. After 20 minutes, check the rice in the pot by taking one or two in fingertips and pressing it gently, whether it is boiled properly or not. If not, then check the rice now and then again.
  4. When boiled, take a bowl, place it somewhere, Tilt the pot, and drain the excess water. If there is no excess water remaining, then ok. After draining, serve the rice in place, and enjoy the rice when the vapour is gone. Or simply start eating RIGHT AWAY!
